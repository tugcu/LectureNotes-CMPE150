Drawing Circles with Input

The initial radius can be specified by the user:

import turtle

radius = int (input("Please enter the initial radius: "))
turtle.speed(8)
turtle.pensize(5)

turtle.pencolor("Green")
turtle.circle(radius)

radius += 10
turtle.pencolor("Blue")
turtle.circle(radius)

radius += 10
